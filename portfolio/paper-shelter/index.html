paper shelter
Year : 2014
Status : Built
Client : AA Visiting School NYC
Location : NYC & Auburn, AL
Roles : Lead Designer, Fabricator, Assembler
A material research project investigating curved-folding to achieve single surface structural rigidity. In this design process the inherent material structures defined themselves, shaped according to rigidity of the folding techniques to define its form.
